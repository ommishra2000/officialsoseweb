The Shaheed Bhagat Singh - Armed Forces Preparatory School (SBS-AFPS), Delhi is a flagship project of the Delhi state government aiming to bring fruition to the dreams of many students filled with zeal and enthusiasm for serving the nation through the armed forces. AFPS is an institute dedicated to training and preparing students from the 9th to 12th grades for admission to the National Defense Academy (NDA) and the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force, as well as other uniformed services.

Why SBS-AFPS?

Rigorous preparation for NDA and its equivalent examination

Exclusive Services Preparatory Wing to inculcate Officer Like Qualities in students

Expert faculty comprising of retired Armed Forces officers and trained instructors

Free co-educational school with separate residential facilities for boys and girls
