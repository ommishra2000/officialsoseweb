Performing and Visual Arts Ambedkar SOSEs (PVA Ambedkar SOSEs) enable a well-rounded education experience - best suited for students who are passionate about arts and keen to eventually pursue a successful career in the field not just as performing artists but also in roles related to creative industries. Students can specialise in their chosen art form and study one of the three art forms offered - Music (Indian and Western), Visual Arts and Film, Acting & Media Studies. The uniquely crafted curriculum and exposure offered open doors for higher education and career pathways in the chosen field of art by the support of guidance, hands-on training, and one-on-one mentoring.

Why PVA ASoSE?
